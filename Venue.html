The Chateau is an exclusive destination at Maple street since 2000 for you to arrange your pre-wedding parties, receptions, weddings and parties. This allows you to select your type of party, the food you want to be served and the theme you want to have for your party and so on. We also offer other services like DJ, Photography and other necessary services as per customer's interest.

We've been in the industry since 10 years and never ever was even one customer left unsatisfied and we make it a point that each of our customer's needs are satisfied and at the end of the day, may it be a small gathering or a party or a wedding, we assure that the customer's requirements are satisfied and their guest are contented with the function.

Our services includes dining (including welcome drink, refreshments, chaat and special fine dining for the couple's family), photography & videography, backdrop arrangement (based on the theme), floral decoration, return gift arrangement, beautician (for the bride and groom) and so on. We also make sure that atleast two men from our team will assist you throughout the function and will make sure your event becomes a grand success.
